This is our rendition of Super maio.
In order to control mario, click on the buttons on the screen.
To move Mario to the right or left, click and hold the right and left arrows for their respective direction. 
To make Mario jump, click the right button on the left side once. You cannot jump again while jumping.
To make Mario shoot a fireball, press the right button on the left side. You cannot shoot 2 fireballs at once.
Killing monsters gives you 125 points to your score. Picking up a coin gives you 100 and picking up a mushroom gives you 150.
You only have 3 lives and if you touch a monster you will lose a life.
